Interesting facts about tarija

  • Tarija has a very pleasant temperate climate, it is constantly warm.
  • The people of Tarija speak Spanish with a unique accent and incorporate a regional vocabulary
  • Tarija is surrounded by fertile valleys and vineyards, making it an ideal region for wine production.
  • Tarija is famous for its traditional music and dance, including the lively “cueca,” a popular Bolivian dance.

Festivities in Tarija

  • New Year: January 1st.
  • Carnivals: (Movable) February - March.
  • Civic Anniversary: April 15th.
  • Easter: April 16th.
  • Festival of the Virgin of Chaguaya: August 15th.
  • Feast of San Roque: First Week of September.
  • Lapacho Festival: October 4th, 5th, and 6th.
  • Christmas: December 24th and 25th.
